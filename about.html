About Julie Brown

The Law Office of Julie A. Brown has been providing quality legal services to the greater San Diego area for over 28 years. Attorney Brown, a former Olympic track & field athlete, pursues the practice of law with the same energy, detail and competitive mindset that drove her to the top of her sport.

The Law Office of Julie A. Brown offers top quality legal expertise with a genuine personal commitment to each client’s needs. We pledge to seek the most efficient and favorable means to achieve our client’s objective with a just outcome.

The Law Office of Julie A. Brown is dedicated to the following areas of practice:

  • Adoptions
  • Dissolution of Marriage
  • Family Law Mediation
